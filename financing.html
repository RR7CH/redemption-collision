Auto Repair Financing FAQ — Humble, TX

What financing options do you offer for auto repair in Humble, TX?

We work with multiple lenders to offer financing up to $5,000 for auto body, collision, and mechanical repair. Options vary based on your credit profile, but we have lenders that work with all credit types — good, bad, and no credit history. Call us at (346) 471-5322 to discuss what's available for your situation.

Is there a minimum repair amount required to finance?

Minimum amounts may vary by lender. Call us and tell us the repair you need — we'll let you know right away whether financing applies and what your options are. For most collision and body repairs, financing is available.

How do I apply for auto repair financing?

Call us at (346) 471-5322 or come in for a free estimate. Once we have the repair cost, we'll walk you through the financing application. The process is quick — most customers complete it in under 15 minutes and get a decision the same day. You'll need a valid ID and basic personal information to apply.

Can I finance collision repair through insurance and out-of-pocket together?

Yes. A common scenario is your insurance covering the bulk of the repair while you finance your deductible or any amount not covered. We help you understand exactly what insurance will pay and what portion — if any — you're responsible for, so you can decide whether financing makes sense for your situation.

Does bad credit disqualify me from financing auto repairs?

No. We specifically work with lenders that accommodate bad credit and limited credit history. Terms and approval amounts may differ from prime credit customers, but we have options for most situations. The best way to find out what you qualify for is to call us and apply — there's no obligation until you accept an offer.
