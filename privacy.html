Privacy Policy

How we protect your personal information.

Last updated: June 16, 2026

1. Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly to us when you:

  • Fill out our contact or estimate request form
  • Call or email our shop
  • Visit our shop in person

This may include your name, phone number, email address, vehicle information, and a description of the services you need.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information solely to:

  • Provide estimates and schedule repairs
  • Communicate with you about your vehicle
  • Process insurance claims on your behalf
  • Send follow-up satisfaction surveys (optional)

3. Information Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We only share information with:

  • Your insurance company (as required for claim processing)
  • Parts suppliers (for ordering correct components)
  • Service providers who assist our operations (e.g., towing partners)

4. Data Security

We implement reasonable security measures to protect your personal information. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure.

5. Your Rights

You may request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal information by contacting us at redemptioncollisioncenter@gmail.com.

6. Cookies

Our website does not use tracking cookies. We may use essential cookies for basic site functionality.
