Meet Thomas

Owner & Master Technician · Humble, TX

Thomas built Redemption Collision Center from the ground up in New Orleans starting in 2011 — nothing but skill, a relentless work ethic, and one standard: treat every car like your own and every customer like family. He spent over a decade earning his name in NOLA before making the move to Humble, TX two years ago, bringing everything he built with him.

Thomas doesn't manage from behind a desk. When a vehicle comes in, he's the one assessing the damage, he's the one in the paint booth, and he's the one making sure the work is right before your keys go back in your hand. That personal accountability is what keeps Humble drivers coming back — and referring their friends.

As a Black-owned, family-operated business, Redemption came to Humble not because it was easy, but because this community needed a shop that would actually do right by people. Thomas will walk you through every step of the repair, give you an honest number upfront, and never send you somewhere else for something he can handle right here.
