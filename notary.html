Frequently Asked Questions

What documents do I need to bring?

Bring a valid government-issued photo ID (driver's license or passport), the document(s) you need notarized, and any supporting paperwork related to the transaction. Don't sign documents before you arrive — notarization requires signing in the notary's presence.

Can you help with a private vehicle sale?

Yes. We can notarize the title transfer and bill of sale for a private vehicle sale. Both buyer and seller should be present with valid ID. Call ahead to make sure we're available when you plan to come in.
